How Space Racing Works:
  • Get in your speedy rockets

    Get ready and do not forget to wear your space suits for safety!

  • Fly 3 laps through the Milky Way race course

    Follow the race-course that is on your on-board rocket computer.

  • Cross the finish line at Earth

    You must enter the Earth atmosphere to complete the race. Be careful because it can get very hot!
